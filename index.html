About

Anastasios Tsakonas is a certified Geologist with a specialization in Engineering Geology and a Master’s degree in Geosciences and Environment from the University of Patras. With extensive experience in geotechnical investigations and studies, he has contributed to major infrastructure projects in Greece and abroad, combining hands-on expertise with technical knowledge. Throughout his career, he has undertaken the supervision of geotechnical investigations, geological mapping, and slope stabilization analyses, utilizing advanced software and conducting precise field and laboratory tests.

Focusing on quality and safety, Anastasios Tsakonas delivers comprehensive services to optimize infrastructure project designs and solve various geological challenges. His experience includes supervising drilling operations, performing in situ tests (SPT, Pocket Penetrometer, Permeability tests), and using software like AutoCAD and Civil 3D to visualize the geological information (maps, cross-sections, etc.) needed for each project. His adaptability and ability to collaborate with teams and clients make him a reliable professional known for his integrity and commitment to delivering high-quality services.
